# Post-Salah Adhkar (Remembrance After Prayer)

_[ANNOUNCE after Tasleem]_ "Let us now recite the post-prayer adhkar."

## Istighfar
**Astaghfirullah** × 3  
_"I ask Allah for forgiveness"_

## Tasbih, Tahmid, Takbir (×33 each)
1. **Subhanallah** × 33 — "Glory be to Allah"
2. **Alhamdulillah** × 33 — "All praise is for Allah"
3. **Allahu Akbar** × 33 — "Allah is the Greatest"

Then once:  
**La ilaha illallahu wahdahu la sharika lah, lahul-mulku wa lahul-hamdu wa huwa 'ala kulli shay'in qadir.**  
_"There is no god but Allah alone with no partner. To Him belongs all sovereignty and praise, and He is over all things capable."_

## Ayat al-Kursi (Surah Al-Baqarah 2:255)
_[RECITE ALOUD — slowly and clearly]_  
Allahu la ilaha illa huwal-hayyul-qayyum. La ta'khudhuhu sinatun wa la nawm...  
_(recite full verse)_

## Closing Dua
Allahumma a'inni 'ala dhikrika wa shukrika wa husni 'ibadatik.  
_"O Allah, help me to remember You, to thank You, and to worship You in the best manner."_

_[ANNOUNCE]_ "Ameen. May Allah accept from all of us. As-salamu alaykum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh."
