## Opening — Niyyah (Intention)
_[ANNOUNCE]_ "Make your intention in your heart to pray [Prayer Name] for the sake of Allah."

## Takbir-ul-Ihram (Opening Takbir)
**Arabic:** اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ  
**Transliteration:** Allahu Akbar  
**Meaning:** Allah is the Greatest  
_[VOICE CUE]_ Raise hands to earlobes, then fold right over left on chest.

---

## Thana (Opening Supplication — recited silently after Takbir)
**Transliteration:**  
Subhanakal-lahumma wa bihamdika wa tabarakas-muka wa ta'ala jadduka wa la ilaha ghayruk.  
**Meaning:** Glory be to You O Allah, and praise. Blessed is Your name and exalted is Your majesty. There is no god but You.

---

## Ta'awwudh
**Transliteration:** A'udhu billahi minash-shaytanir rajim  
**Meaning:** I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Satan.

---

## Surah Al-Fatiha (Every Rakat)
**Transliteration:**
1. Bismillahir-Rahmanir-Rahim
2. Al-hamdu lillahi Rabbil 'alamin
3. Ar-Rahmanir-Rahim
4. Maliki yawmid-din
5. Iyyaka na'budu wa iyyaka nasta'in
6. Ihdinas-siratal-mustaqim
7. Siratal-ladhina an'amta 'alayhim ghayril-maghdubi 'alayhim wa lad-dallin

_[ANNOUNCE]_ "Ameen" — congregation responds.

---

## Surah (Rakats 1 & 2 only — example: Al-Ikhlas)
**Transliteration:**  
Qul huwa Allahu ahad. Allahu as-Samad. Lam yalid wa lam yulad. Wa lam yakun lahu kufuwan ahad.

---

## Ruku (Bowing)
_[VOICE CUE]_ "Allahu Akbar" — bow with hands on knees, back straight.  
**Tasbih (×3):** Subhana Rabbiyal Adhim — "How perfect is my Lord, the Magnificent"

## Qiyam (Rising from Ruku)
_[VOICE]_ Sami'Allahu liman hamidah — "Allah hears the one who praises Him"  
_[VOICE]_ Rabbana walakal hamd — "Our Lord, all praise is for You"

---

## Sujud (Prostration)
_[VOICE CUE]_ "Allahu Akbar" — forehead, nose, both palms, both knees, and toes on floor.  
**Tasbih (×3):** Subhana Rabbiyal A'la — "How perfect is my Lord, the Most High"

## Julus (Sitting Between Two Sujuds)
_[VOICE CUE]_ "Allahu Akbar" — sit briefly.  
**Dua:** Rabbighfirli — "O my Lord, forgive me"

## Second Sujud
_[VOICE CUE]_ "Allahu Akbar" — prostrate again.  
**Tasbih (×3):** Subhana Rabbiyal A'la

---

## Tashahud (After 2nd rakat of any prayer)
**Transliteration:**  
At-Tahiyyatu lillahi was-salawatu wat-tayyibat.  
As-salamu alayka ayyuhan-Nabiyyu wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh.  
As-salamu alayna wa 'ala 'ibadillahis-salihin.  
Ash-hadu alla ilaha illallah, wa ash-hadu anna Muhammadan 'abduhu wa rasuluh.  

**Action:** Raise right index finger when saying "illa Allah".

---

## Salawat Ibrahim (Final Tashahud only)
**Transliteration:**  
Allahumma salli 'ala Muhammadin wa 'ala ali Muhammad,  
kama sallayta 'ala Ibrahima wa 'ala ali Ibrahim, innaka Hamidun Majid.  
Allahumma barik 'ala Muhammadin wa 'ala ali Muhammad,  
kama barakta 'ala Ibrahima wa 'ala ali Ibrahim, innaka Hamidun Majid.

---

## Tasleem (Ending the Prayer)
_[VOICE CUE]_ Turn face to the **right** and say:  
**As-salamu alaykum wa rahmatullah**  
_[VOICE CUE]_ Turn face to the **left** and say:  
**As-salamu alaykum wa rahmatullah**  

_[ANNOUNCE]_ "The prayer is complete. Alhamdulillah."

## Prayer-by-Prayer Script

### FAJR (2 Rakats — Aloud)
- Rakat 1: Takbir → Thana → Fatiha → Surah → Ruku → Sujud x2
- Rakat 2: Takbir → Fatiha → Surah → Ruku → Sujud x2 → Tashahud → Salawat → Tasleem

### DHUHR / ASR (4 Rakats — Silent)
- Rakats 1-2: Full as above → Tashahud (short, no Salawat)
- Rakats 3-4: Fatiha only (no extra Surah), silent → Final Tashahud + Salawat → Tasleem

### MAGHRIB (3 Rakats)
- Rakats 1-2: Aloud → Tashahud (short)
- Rakat 3: Silent → Final Tashahud + Salawat → Tasleem

### ISHA (4 Rakats)
- Rakats 1-2: Aloud → Tashahud (short)
- Rakats 3-4: Silent → Final Tashahud + Salawat → Tasleem
